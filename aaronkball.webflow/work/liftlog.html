Project Overview
"Strength in numbers"

‍LiftLog is an intuitive weight lifting progress tracking app that offers pre-existing programs and custom workout builders to meet fitness goals. It tracks weight lifted, sets and reps, and creates reports to visualize progress. Suitable for all fitness levels, LiftLog is an essential tool for fitness enthusiasts.
My Contributions
For weight lifters following a program, the traditional habit of keeping track of progress with a notepad or spreadsheet can be a time-consuming and inefficient process. Many lifters struggle to maintain consistency and accuracy in their tracking, leading to stalled fitness goals. Traditional tracking also makes it difficult to review past workouts and visualise progress.
