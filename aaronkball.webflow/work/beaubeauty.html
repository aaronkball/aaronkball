Project Overview
Beau Beauty Salon is a new family-owned business in the beauty industry, which gave me the opportunity to design a complete brand identity, website, business cards, and price list.
My Contributions
The salon was looking for a clean and modern aesthetic that would appeal to a predominantly female target audience, with a color scheme of pink and white.The end result is a fresh and airy design that perfectly reflects the values of Beau Beauty Salon and its commitment to providing high-quality beauty services.You can view the Beau Beauty website here.
