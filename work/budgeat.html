Project Overview
"Budget your meals. Savour the savings."
‍
‍Budgeat is a user-friendly meal planning application that empowers users to discover and organize recipes that align with their dietary preferences and weekly food budget.
The Problem
It requires time and effort to research recipes and coordinate meal schedules. Individuals often have to account for dietary restrictions and budget constraints, which can add an extra layer of complexity to the meal planning process. The lack of proper planning can result in poor food choices, overspending on groceries, and food waste.
