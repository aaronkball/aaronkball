PORTFOLIO CONTACT
Project Overview
Faceoff is a skincare brand that prides itself on using only natural and sustainable ingredients in their products. My role was to create a visual identity that reflected the brand's core values and unique offerings.
My Contributions
I was tasked with designing the logo, packaging, and stationery for Faceoff, which presented an exciting opportunity to develop a cohesive and impactful brand presence. Through my design process, I aimed to create a look and feel that was not only visually appealing, but also communicated the brand's commitment to natural, ethical skincare.
